Objective

What this workflow was built to answer

🔍 The Problem

A team of 10 people needs to choose a restaurant for an evening dinner in Thonglor, Bangkok. Instead of relying on subjective opinions or random Google searches, this workflow uses real scraped data, AI normalization, and a structured 100-point scoring model to surface the objectively best options.

✅ The Decision Question

If the team must choose 1–3 restaurants from Thonglor for a post-work team dinner, which options score highest across: review quality, group suitability, price, travel convenience, data completeness, and uniqueness of experience?

Decision Criteria: Group-friendly seating · Budget ≤ ฿500/person · Within 1km of BTS Thonglor · Open for dinner (18:00–22:00) · Evidence-backed recommendations
