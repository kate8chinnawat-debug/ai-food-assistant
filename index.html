Objective

Decision question this workflow was built to answer

🔍 The Problem

A team of 10 people needs a restaurant for an evening dinner in Thonglor, Bangkok. This AI workflow uses real scraped data from Google Maps (Apify) and Wongnai, normalizes it with Claude AI, scores it with a 100-point weighted model, and automates scoring with n8n.

✅ Decision Question

From 21 real restaurants in Thonglor, which 1–3 score highest across: rating quality, group suitability, price, travel convenience, data completeness, and uniqueness — for a team of 8–12 people?

Decision Criteria: Group-friendly seating · Budget ≤ ฿500/person · Within 1km of BTS Thonglor · Open for dinner (18:00–22:00) · Evidence-backed with real scraped data
